About Global Happiness

Global Happiness is a visualization of the World Happiness Report 2021.


The world map is colored according to each country's Ladder Score, a numerical value representing the degree to which people feel happy with their lives.


Hover over each country to learn about the factors that contribute to its Ladder Score. Zoom in and out to view the map more clearly.


All data was sourced from Kaggle.
